Dancing throughout the Years

Dance is one of the main ways I stay creative and active at the University of Maryland and since I was a child. I currently dance on Maryland Dhoom, a Bollywood fusion team, and enjoy learning choreography, performing, and building community. I have been dancing since I was 2 years old and have been on various teams and taken classes in different styles, from ballet to hip hop to indian classical. Dance has been a constant source of joy and growth for me, and I look forward to continuing to dance throughout my life either at weddings or at competitions on a stage.
